Philosophers dinner

Multithreading implementation of solution of the Dining Philosophers Problem, but this time our philosophers are reading while eating. Dinner ends when everybody read all of the books. Each philospher choose book he hadn't already read, next choose seat and try to get forks. All choices are random. Waiter watches over the dinner and allows max four people to the table at the time.

Decide how many books are on the bookcase and adjust how long philosophers eat, duration of other actions will change accordingly. You can also choose if waiter should be present, but be careful - it might end bad.
